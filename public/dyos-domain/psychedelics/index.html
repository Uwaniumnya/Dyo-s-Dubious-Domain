Understanding Psychedelics

üß† Mechanisms of Action

Most classical psychedelics (LSD, psilocybin, mescaline, DMT) work primarily as 5-HT2A serotonin receptor agonists. This disrupts normal brain network communication, leading to altered perception and consciousness.

‚ö†ÔøΩEÔøΩECommon Risks

  • Psychological distress: Bad trips, anxiety, panic attacks
  • HPPD: Hallucinogen Persisting Perception Disorder
  • Triggering latent conditions: Can precipitate psychotic episodes in vulnerable individuals
  • Dangerous behavior: Poor judgment during the experience
  • Emotional amplification: Existing mental health issues may be intensified

üõ°ÔøΩEÔøΩESet, Setting & Safety

  • Set: Your mindset - be in a good mental space, well-rested
  • Setting: Safe, comfortable environment with trusted people
  • Sitter: Have a sober, experienced person present for high doses
  • No mixing: Avoid combining psychedelics, especially with alcohol
  • Mental health: Don't use if you have active psychotic disorders
  • Integration: Process the experience afterward with journaling or therapy

üìö Therapeutic Potential

Psychedelics are experiencing a renaissance in psychiatric research. Studies show promise for treating depression, PTSD, addiction, and end-of-life anxiety. Psilocybin and MDMA are advancing through FDA trials.
