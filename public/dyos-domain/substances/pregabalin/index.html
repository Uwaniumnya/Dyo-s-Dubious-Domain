By binding to this subunit, it reduces calcium influx at nerve terminals, thereby decreasing the release of excitatory neurotransmitters including glutamate, norepinephrine, and substance P. Cultural Impact

Pregabalin represents a shift in anxiety treatment but also highlighted the ongoing tension between therapeutic benefit and abuse potential. Its euphoric effects made it popular in recreational drug communities, leading to increased regulatory scrutiny and concerns about prescription drug abuse.
