üß† Real Pharmacology & Biochemistry

Biochemical Origin & Function

Adrenochrome is simply the oxidation product of epinephrine (adrenaline). It forms naturally in the human body through normal metabolic processes. When adrenaline is exposed to oxygen, it spontaneously oxidizes to form adrenochrome and other metabolites. This happens in everyone's bloodstream as a normal part of catecholamine metabolism.
