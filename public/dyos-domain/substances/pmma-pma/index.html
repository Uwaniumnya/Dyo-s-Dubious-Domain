üß† Pharmacology & Neuroscience

Primary Mechanism of Action

PMMA and PMA are potent monoamine releasing agents with particularly strong serotonin release activity. Unlike MDMA, they have much weaker dopaminergic activity and significantly stronger serotonergic effects, leading to dangerous hyperthermia. They also have significant alpha-adrenergic activity, causing cardiovascular complications.

No therapeutic window: Margin between effects and lethality is minimal
